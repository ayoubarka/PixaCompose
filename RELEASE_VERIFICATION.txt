================================================================================

   - Weekly frequency mode (multi-select weekdays)
   - Monthly frequency mode (multi-select days)
   - Full shape customization
   - Optional/nullable labels

   - 12+ extracted composable functions
   - SchedulePicker demonstrations
   - NavigationDemoScreen with Drawer demo
